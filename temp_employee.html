Employee Profile:

Carm Espinal

Carm has been with Fleming GIS ConsultingTM since their conception in 1998. Pursuing a lifelong passion for nature, they graduated from the Forestry Technician prohram at Sault College in 2020, planning on working in forest fire for the remainder of their life. After 8 months their knees disagreed and in 2021 they decided to pursue a more chair-based career in GIS, enrolling in the Fleming College's GIS program. Their love for the natural world has lead them to work out of a remote property in northern Ontario, where they plan to garden, go fishing, and admire the trees during the day and keep their capitalist GIS pursuits to the night-time when the trees are hard to see. They would love to bring their cartographic skills to the table to enrich your next project!

  • ArcGIS Pro: Proficient in the creation of physical maps, with breadth and possibilities only limited by the data at hand
  • Python: Will probably pass their python course
  • Adobe Illustrator: Adept at utilizing Ai's MaPublisher to enhance mapping data
  • Graduated Fleming College's GIS program at the top of their class, with a 1.8 GPA
  • Highly skilled in Ontario tree and shrub identification
  • Proficient in use of navigation technology in the field, including basic map/compass and GPS technology
  • Can speak to trees and also mosses

Past Projects

Kirkland Lake Trail Atlas

August 2021 - Present

Utilizing their newfound GIS skills, Carm is in the process of creating an extensive all-season trail map of Kirkland Lake, Ontario. Through this solo project, they will be collecting open-source data to create initial navigation maps, using those maps to traverse the area over many seasons, and then publishing a newly designed and extensive map using their findings.
